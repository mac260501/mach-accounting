Why Estate Planning Can't Wait

The Three Things That Happen When There's No Plan

Estates without proper planning don't just create legal headaches ‚Äî they erode the wealth you spent a lifetime building and fracture the families you were trying to protect.

01

Probate Delays & Costs

Without proper trust structures, estates can be tied up in probate for months or years ‚Äî with fees eating up to 3% or more of your total estate value before a single dollar reaches your family.

02

CRA Takes More Than Necessary

Unplanned estates trigger capital gains and income inclusions on death that could have been legally minimized. Poor structure means CRA becomes one of your largest "beneficiaries."

03

Family Conflict

Ambiguous wills and poorly structured estates are the single largest driver of family disputes. Clear, professional planning eliminates uncertainty before it becomes irreversible damage.
